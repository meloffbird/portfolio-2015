The App

dsidr’s minimal interface and playful interactions allow users to easily add ideas and have those ideas randomly shuffled, producing a random answer, similar to the old tradition of throwing paper into a hat. Ideal for settling debates about what to eat for lunch, what movie to watch at Tuesday movie night and for any other moment when you just can’t make up your mind!

The app started out as an iPhone application but in order to meet deadlines for the MVP we decided to change it to a iPad/web app.
