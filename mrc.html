Marcus Savings

Date
2017
Project Role
User Experience

Objective

After Elephant helped to launch Goldman Sachs’ first venture into the consumer-facing market with the personal loan platform Marcus, they returned to us to help transition their recent acquisition, GS Savings Bank, into the Marcus brand. Although we had learned a lot of lessons from the initial Marcus build, this assignment required us to completely rethink the onboarding and logged-in experience in order to develop a more thoughtful big-picture solution. The main ask was to improve the digital experience for current customers while allowing for seamless product expansion in the future.

Along with my UX director, I was the only User Experience Designer on the team and quickly developed a strong working relationship with the client, as we worked closely with their burgeoning internal product team. My responsibilities included working with legal, tech, and fraud leads to develop key product flows as well as collaborating with visual design and development in creating hi-fidelty wireframes for agile sprints.

The project lasted a little over a year and Marcus officially launched in December 2017. This was a fast-moving project with a lot of great challenges that I would love to share and discuss.
